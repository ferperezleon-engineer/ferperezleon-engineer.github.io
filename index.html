About me:

My educational background is a Bachelor's of Science in Industrial Engineering (2017) from New Mexico State University in Las Cruces, NM. After graduating, I spent a year working as a contract Industrial Engineer in the Midwest. I then moved to the San Fernando Valley of southern California and pivoted to media where I joined The Last Podcast Network. I got some great experience as Production Director and as a co-host on the political comedy podcast Abe Lincoln's Top Hat until December 2023. Next, I returned to NM and joined the a major TV network as a Digital Show Producer for KRQE News Channel 13. Here I have had the opportunity to work with some amazingly talented people including Chad Brummett on the Emmy-award winning NM Frontiers show, Investigative reporter Gabrielle Burkhart and Executive producer Chris McKee on their Edward R. Murrow winning New Mexico News Insiders video podcast, award winning anchors Jessica Garate and Dean Staley on the New Mexico Crime Files digital show, and with 30 year sports veteran anchor/reporter Van Tate on several sports coverage shows.
